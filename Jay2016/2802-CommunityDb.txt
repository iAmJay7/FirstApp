Community Database
==================

1. Two types of users/roles - Admin & Member
2. Represent the family and family related data -- Use geneology
3. There are "N" number of "villages/cities" that form a particular community (e.g 52 samaj or 27 )
4. There will be no option to delete a record physically - Use flag to hide/show records from the table (delete/undelete)
5. Set created by and date, updated by and date meta to each table.
